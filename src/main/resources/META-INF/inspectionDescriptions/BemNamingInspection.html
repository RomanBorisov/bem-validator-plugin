Enforces BEM naming conventions for blocks, elements, and modifiers.

Correct usage:

<div class="block-name"></div>
<div class="block-name__element-name"></div>
<div class="block-name__element-name--modifier-name"></div>

Incorrect usage:

<div class="BlockName"></div>
<div class="block__ElementName"></div>
<div class="block__element--ModifierName"></div>
<div class="block___element"></div>
<div class="block--element"></div>
